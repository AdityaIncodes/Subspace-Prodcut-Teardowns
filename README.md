# Subspace Product Teardown

Prepared as part of a Product Intern Assignment.

All observations are based on hands 
on testing of the product.
## Proposed Product Improvements

![Wireframe](product%20wireframe%20image.jpeg)
## Key Findings

---

## 1. Fake Subscription Listings Can Be Created Too Easily

### Evidence

![Netflix Verification](netflix-verification.jpg)

### Observed

I was able to create a Netflix subscription listing using screenshot-based verification.

### Problem

The current flow validates the presence of proof but not necessarily the authenticity of the subscription.

### Ship Instead

- OCR verification
- Ownership verification
- Random proof challenges
- New seller review period

### Expected Impact

- Lower fraud
- Higher buyer trust
- Reduced refund requests

---

## 2. Expand Payment Hold / Escrow System

### Observed

The payment hold system is one of the strongest trust mechanisms in the product.

### Ship Instead

Buyer Pays → Payment Held → Access Confirmed → Release Funds

### Expected Impact

- Lower refund losses
- Better trust
- Reduced platform liability

---

## 3. Subscription Bundles

### Current Experience

![Current Subscription Marketplace](subscription-marketplace.jpg)

### Proposed Experience

![OTT Bundle Example](ott-bundle.jpg)

### Problem

Users evaluate subscriptions individually.

### Ship Instead

Create:

- Entertainment Bundle
- Student Bundle
- Creator Bundle

### Expected Impact

- Higher retention
- Higher average order value
- Simpler buying experience

---

## 4. Clear ICP Focus

Focus onboarding on:

> Students and Young Professionals looking to reduce recurring subscription costs.

---

## 5. Reputation System Needs More Signal

### Current Seller Discovery

![Seller Listings](seller-list.jpg)

### Current Reviews

![Seller Reviews](seller-reviews.jpg)

### Problem

Most sellers have very similar ratings, making differentiation difficult.

### Ship Instead

Display:

- Trust Score
- Successful Transactions
- Success Rate
- Dispute Rate
- Member Since

### Expected Impact

Higher buyer confidence and stronger marketplace trust.

---

## Final Recommendation

The strongest opportunity for Subspace is strengthening marketplace trust.

Priority 1:

- Fake listing prevention
- Expanded escrow workflow

Priority 2:

- Subscription bundles
- Better seller reputation signals

These initiatives can improve buyer confidence while reducing fraud-related losses.
